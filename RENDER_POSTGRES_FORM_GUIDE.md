## How to Fill Out the Form

### Field 1: Name
**Label**: "A unique name for your Postgres instance"  
**Enter**: `student-card-db`  
**Purpose**: This is what you'll see in your Render dashboard

### Field 2: Database (if shown)
**Label**: "Database" or "Database Name"  
**Enter**: `student_card_db`  
**Purpose**: Actual PostgreSQL database name (used in connection strings)

### Field 3: Region (if shown)
**Label**: "Region"  
**Select**: Choose closest to your users (e.g., "Oregon (US West)")

### Field 4: Plan (if shown)
**Label**: "Plan"  
**Select**: **Free** (for development/testing)

---

## Step-by-Step

1. **Name field**: Type `student-card-db`
2. **Database field** (if present): Type `student_card_db`
3. **Region**: Select your preferred region
4. **Plan**: Select **Free**
5. **Click "Create Database"** button
